About Us

ENTITY SAFETY is a South African based company providing Risk Management, Legal Compliance applications, Disaster Management planning and coordination, Risk Assessments and Occupational Health and Safety services servicing the Events Industry, adding value and benefiting our customers through the achievement of compliance in their organizations and obtaining regulatory permissions and authority to stage their events. Overall Safety and Risk consultancy services are also provided for special events, exhibitions, sports meetings and live concerts as well as Conferences (local & international), Venues, Hotels, Local & International World Summits. The Company is a full member of the South African Institute of Occupational Safety and Health (SAIOSH). The expertise developed by the directors and team at ENTITY SAFETY over many years of practical experience is made available to the events industry, together with the extensive resources at the Company's disposal. ENTITY SAFETY is often called upon to provide Safety, Security consultancy and Risk management services simultaneously at several venues throughout South Africa. Legal compliance audits are essential in business today and protection from liability and lawsuits emanating from public events and venues attended by the public are commonplace. ENTITY SAFETY ensures our clients peace of mind.
